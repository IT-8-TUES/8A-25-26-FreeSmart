Jazz

Jazz is often called "America's Classical Music." Originating in New Orleans at the turn of the 20th century, it is defined by its complexity and its focus on improvisation—the art of making music in the moment. It has traveled from the dance halls of the Swing era to the intellectual, fast-paced world of Bebop and the mellow, atmospheric vibes of Cool Jazz.
