Electronic

Electronic music began in laboratories and experimental studios but eventually took over the dance floor. Using synthesizers, drum machines, and computers, this genre removed the need for traditional instruments. Whether it's the robotic precision of Kraftwerk or the massive "drops" of modern EDM, Electronic music is defined by its endless search for new textures and sounds.
