Pop

Short for "Popular Music," Pop is designed for the masses. While it borrows from every other genre, Pop is characterized by its catchy "hooks," high production value, and its ability to reflect the current fashion and technology of the time. From the "Beatlemania" of the 60s to the synth-heavy 80s and the viral-driven hits of the 2020s, Pop is the ever-changing soundtrack of our lives.
