Rock

Born from the fusion of blues, country, and rhythm & blues in the 1950s, rock music has evolved into one of the most influential and diverse genres in history. From the rebellious spirit of early rock and roll to the stadium-filling anthems of classic rock, this genre has continuously reinvented itself while maintaining its raw, electrifying energy.
